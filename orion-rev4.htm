ORION-4

ORION-4 is the Wood Artistry refined, commercial version of ORION-3.5. Don Naple's target market is the traditional audiophile who knows what real music sounds like and who has not yet found the loudspeaker that delivers a fully satisfying illusion of it in his or her home. These are connoisseurs who love music and who recognize that the ORION is unique in its appearance, exceptional in its performance, and of outstanding quality and value.

The ORION-4 product consists of two loudspeakers, two 4-way loudspeaker cables, and an Analog Signal Processor. All parts are matching designs and exhibit superb craftsmanship.  Price $14,950

Add-on options are offered for custom 8-conductor speaker cables with Speakon connectors and for sets of interconnect cables.

A complete ORION-4 sound system requires six or eight channels of power amplification for left and right woofers, midrange and tweeter drivers, plus interconnect cables to connect the ASP to the power amplifiers and the preamplifier output. 

Listen to Don in the AV Showrooms video Orion Loudspeakers, Pass Labs and MSB from AXPONA 2012 in Florida. (#7 from top, PLUTO is playing)

ORION-4 Loudspeaker System . . .
The Window to Recorded Music and Sound in Your Home

 

The loudspeakers disappear, the room is forgotten, and you listen through a wide open window to the performance in front of you. Stereo at its best, an illusion engine that satisfies your aural sensibilities, as it reveals the artistry of the performers and the recording.

ORION-4 is custom built by Wood Artistry. The speaker’s open-baffle and dynamic drivers follow the concept of the praised AA Beethoven and Vivaldi loudspeakers of the late nineties. With newer components and design refinements ORION-4 surpasses their performance.

Uniform sound dispersion over a wide frequency range is an essential characteristic of ORION-4. Thereby reflections from the room’s surfaces have similar spectral content as the direct sound. Placement of the loudspeakers out in the room adds delay to these reflections, allowing the ear-brain hearing apparatus to differentiate between the direct sounds from the loudspeakers and the room’s response. In effect, room and loudspeakers disappear and a phantom auditory scene appears in front of the listener.

Highest quality SEAS tweeter, midrange and woofer drivers are used. They are mounted to minimize mechanical vibration coupling between them. The new long-throw woofers operate in force cancelling opposition. This arrangement reduces mechanical vibration of the cabinet and reduces transfer of vibration energy into the floor, which could cause resonant re-radiation from floor and walls.
