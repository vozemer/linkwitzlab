Honeymoon Cottage

Honeymoon Cottage was designed by a member of the original group of UC Berkeley architects that worked on The Sea Ranch and it exemplifies their philosophy for blending buildings with their natural setting and atmosphere. Honeymoon Cottage belongs to one of the Cluster Homes which border a large meadow that flows down towards the Pacific Ocean.  

 

 View from the sun deck

 

 Living and dining room area as seen from the bedroom loft

 

 The Linkwitz Lab ORION+ loudspeakers have been set up for CD and DVD sound and i-Pod input. 

These loudspeakers exemplify high accuracy sound reproduction in terms of dynamics, timbre, and imaging for 2-channel recordings. Bring your best CD or even your iPod and enjoy. Hear for yourself whether this could be your last loudspeaker system. You have a unique opportunity, because Linkwitz Lab provides all the information to build these speakers yourself. Alternatively, you could acquire them custom built, turn-key, and in the wood of your choice from Wood Artistry.

Hear for yourself whether this is a loudspeaker system that is worth pursuing.
